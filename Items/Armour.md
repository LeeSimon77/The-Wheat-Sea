# Sea of Wheat — Armour

**AC Bonus** adds to base AC. **Hardness** reduces damage from blocked attacks. **Block Charges** are uses before the item needs repair. **Resource** is the minimum Resource Level to acquire.

Weight keywords: `Weight` = counts as one weight category heavier; `Weightx2` = two categories heavier. `Civilian` = passes as ordinary clothing.

### Descriptions

**Padded Doublet** — A short jacket or jack of quilted fabric, leather, or gambeson that provides decent protection to the chest and sometimes upper arms. Comes in many forms; will often have attachment points for additional armour.

**Full Padded Gambeson** — A full-sleeve jacket of thick quilted fabric and leather. Matching padded chausse cover the waist and legs.

**Chain Shirt** — A short shirt of interlocked mail that protects the torso.

**Chain Hauberk with Gambeson** — A thigh-length jacket of metal mail with long sleeves, worn overtop of gambeson, a doublet, or other padded clothes. Usually worn as the main protective armour.

**Full Gambeson and Chain** — A long-sleeve jacket, chausse, and cap of thick quilted fabric, reinforced in key spots with mail to protect vital areas from strikes that avoid the main armour.

## Main Armour

| Scale Mail Cuirass | +4 | 8 | 4 | Weightx2 | Stealth (disadvantage) | 4 |
| Breastplate | +2 | 10 | 2 | Weight | — | 4 |
| Brigandine Cuirass | +4 | 6 | 6 | Weightx2 | — | 4+ |
| Splint Cuirass | +4 | 8 | 4 | Weightx2 | Stealth (disadvantage) | 4+ |
| Plate Cuirass | +5 | 10 | 6 | Weightx2 | Stealth (disadvantage) | 6 |

### Descriptions

**Boiled Leather Cuirass** — Thick leather treated to make a stiff but lightweight torso and waist armour.

**Lacquered Wood Cuirass** — Wooden pieces attached to a leather or cloth backing; cheap and durable torso armour.

**Scale Mail Cuirass** — Metal scales overlapped and interlinked for a durable and flexible torso and waist armour.

**Breastplate** — A metal plate or set of plates that fully protect the front torso of the wearer.

**Brigandine Cuirass** — Large metal strips riveted between sheets of leather; cheap and easily repairable.

**Splint Cuirass** — A reinforced leather-padded cuirass with interlocking metal lames around the torso; flexible but protective.

**Plate Cuirass** — A full set of interlocking metal plates providing maximum protection. Includes breastplate, backplate, placard, tassets, and faulds.

**Half Helmet** — A protective helmet covering at least half the wearer's head.

**Full Helmet** — Fully covers the wearer's head but leaves the face open.

**Visored Helmet** — A full helmet with a visor the wearer can use an action to raise or lower. Visor down grants the higher AC bonus at the cost of perception disadvantage.
