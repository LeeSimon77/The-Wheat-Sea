# Items

Each player has a **Carry Capacity**, this value represents the total amount of things you can declare that you have on your person at any one time.

**Carry Capacity = 2 + 2 X CON mod**

Between adventures and while a character does not need items, they do not have to declare *What* they are carrying, as a sensible adventurer it is assumed that they pack what they need. When a player wants an item, they mark its **Item Capacity** off their **Carry Capacity** and they now have the item. The Item must be at least 1 resource level lower then their current resource level to automatically declare that they have it. A player can declare that they have an item equal to their resource level however they have to expend **1 Flashback Point**, then make a Resource check as normal.

Once a players carry capacity is full they cannot declare any new items until they could feesably restock. 

If a player gains an item during an adventure they must have the appropriate amount of carry capacity to keep it, a player may declare that they are leaving a predeclared item behind to aquire the new item.
